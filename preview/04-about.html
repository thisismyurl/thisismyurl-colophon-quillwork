Quillwork is an independent design studio founded in 2018. We specialise in visual identity and editorial design for publishers, cultural institutions, and independent makers whose work demands permanence.

We are a small, intentionally so. Every project is led by a senior designer from brief to delivery. We take on eight to twelve projects a year. If you are reading this, there is almost certainly a wait — and we believe the work is worth it.
